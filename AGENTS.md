## Global portfolio constraints
- Prioritize premium portfolio storytelling over dashboard-like chrome.
- Keep industrial inspiration subtle; do not style as an internal admin console.
- Maintain technical credibility and senior-level tone.
- Avoid generic recruiter or marketing language.
- Emphasize architecture, integration, deployment, ownership, and engineering impact.
- Keep project summaries concise and realistic.

## Precedence rules (when constraints conflict)
- Correctness and accessibility over visual polish.
- Maintainability and reuse over one-off styling shortcuts.
- Storytelling clarity over decorative UI chrome.
- Simplicity over premature abstraction.

## Traceability requirements
- Append each implementation session to `DEVELOPMENT_LOG.md`.
- Keep task status current in `TASK_PROGRESS.md`.
- Note deviations from the implementation plan when they occur.
